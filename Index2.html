How I
Work

My work focuses on executive decision-making, operating model clarity, and governance readiness. I do not operate as an implementer or program manager.

I partner with senior leaders and boards at moments where judgment, alignment, and risk management matter most — where the cost of misalignment exceeds the cost of delay.

How I
Engage

Three focused engagement models, each designed for a specific leadership challenge.

01
Enterprise Technology
Operating Model
Review

A focused review of how technology actually operates across the enterprise, assessed against business strategy, risk posture, and growth objectives.

  • Helps executive teams and boards understand whether their current structure, decision rights, and incentives are enabling progress or quietly increasing exposure.
  • Outcome is a concise executive brief that clarifies structural risks, sources of leverage, and leadership-level actions required to strengthen reliability and accountability.
02
Executive Decision
Support for
Large-Scale Change

Strategic advisory support for senior leaders navigating high-stakes technology decisions where the cost of misalignment exceeds the cost of delay.

  • Pressure-test assumptions, surface second-order impacts, and evaluate tradeoffs across platform strategy, sourcing models, and organizational change.
  • Designed to strengthen decision quality and executive confidence during transformation, without introducing additional delivery complexity.
03
Board-Level
Technology & Risk
Readiness

Support for executive teams preparing to engage boards on technology posture, investment decisions, and enterprise risk.

  • Translate complex technical realities into clear governance narratives, enabling boards to ask the right questions and executives to communicate with clarity and credibility.
  • The focus is not compliance or reporting, but shared understanding and durable oversight that enables sustained progress.
