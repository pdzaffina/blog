Prompt: "I've created this [email/presentation/strategy/document]: [paste your work].

Tell me exactly what's holding it back from being a 10. Then rewrite it to fix those issues.
After that, critique your rewrite and improve it one more time.
Show me the progression and explain what made each version better."
